Pin the extension

  1. Click the Extensions icon (puzzle) in the top‑right corner of Chrome.
  2. Find Password Lock in the list.
  3. Click the pin icon — it turns blue when pinned.

Set your master password

Click the Password Lock icon in the toolbar. Create a strong master password and confirm it. You’ll use it to lock/unlock the extension.

  • Use 12+ characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Do not reuse your email or banking passwords.
  • Store it safely — if you forget it, we cannot recover it.
